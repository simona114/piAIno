## Piano sound samples

**Salamander Grand Piano** by Alexander Holm
License: [Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported (CC BY 3.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/)
Original recordings: https://archive.org/details/SalamanderGrandPianoV3
Downloaded via: https://github.com/darosh/samples-piano-mp3

30 of the 88 keys (A0, C8, and every note 3 semitones apart in between) play a directly
recorded sample. The remaining keys are pitch-shifted from the nearest recorded sample using
Android's `SoundPool` playback rate, never more than 1 semitone away from its source recording.
